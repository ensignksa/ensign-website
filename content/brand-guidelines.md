# Ensign AI Labs — Brand Guidelines

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Neue Machina
- **Sub-headlines:** Montserrat

## Tagline
AI Systems. Human Leadership.

## Brand Colors
- Blue: #6B9BF7 (cornflower blue)
- Black: #000000
- White: #FFFFFF
- Yellow: #F5D84E
- Coral/Red: ~#F05A5A
- Gradient: blue/red/orange multicolor

## Logo Variants
- Full wordmark on blue background
- Full wordmark on black background (white logo)
- Full wordmark on white background (black logo)
- Full wordmark on gradient background
- Full wordmark on coral/red background
- Full wordmark on yellow background
- Icon only (E mark) on white background
- Icon only (E mark) on black background (white icon)
