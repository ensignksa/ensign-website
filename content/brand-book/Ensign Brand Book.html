Ensign · Brand Book

Voice & tone.

Ensign sounds like a senior practitioner who has done the work. Not the consultant. Not the salesperson. The one who actually shipped it.

Calm.

No exclamation marks. No urgency theatre. Confidence reads as quiet, not loud. If the work needs adjectives to sound impressive, the work is not impressive.

Exact.

Specific numbers, named outcomes, concrete dates. "Last quarter" beats "recently". "+18%" beats "significant lift". When you cannot be exact, do not write.

Quietly bold.

One strong claim per piece. Short sentences. The bold thing earns the room because everything around it is plain.

We say

"Strategy you can ship on Monday."

Specific timeframe. Verb-led. No jargon.

We don't say

"Synergistic solutions to drive impactful business transformation."

Unmeasurable. Adjective-stacked. Hollow.
